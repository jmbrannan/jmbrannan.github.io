Pan Baked Mixed Veggies


Baking pan filled with oven-baked seasoned veggies

A fantastic side dish made from delicious pan baked veggies

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • Broccoli
  • Squash
  • Zucchini
  • Carrots
  • Ground Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Clean the area and prepare a cutting board
  3. Cut all the vegetables into bite sized pieces.
  4. Place cut veggies onto a baking pan
  5. Drizzle a generous amount of olive oil on all veggies.
  6. Add salt and pepper or other spices (according to personal preference)
  7. Cook in oven for 25 minutes* or until veggies are cooked crispy.

Check every 5-10 minutes
