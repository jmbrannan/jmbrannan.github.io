Grilled Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwiches


Super tasty and easy to make grilled P B & J's

Yum Yum PB&J in my Tum Tum

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Spread approximately 2 tablespoons of peanut butter on a slice of bread
  2. Spread approximately 2 tablespoons of jelly on another slice of bread
  3. Place butter in pan
  4. Cook both sides of sandwich until crispy
